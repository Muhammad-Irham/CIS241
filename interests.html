  • Fresh Water Aquariums

  • Ever since covid-19 struck, I've been bored at home and have found myself looking for things to do. I happened to walk past the fish aisle during a routine trip to petsmart for cat supplies, and decided to pick up a Betta fish out of curiousity. After buying the betta fish randomly, I researched more and more about the hobby and bought plants for the aquarium once in a while. If you are in the Seattle area, I highly suggest visiting Aquarium Coop, they have a large selection of fresh water fish, and their website contains helpful tips on how to start or care for an aquarium.
